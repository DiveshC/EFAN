Bio

In brief, EFAN is a student run charity aimed at improving the life style of the people occupying a orphanage that TFSS adopted 2 years ago in Northern Uganda. This year we plan to build 2 classrooms with a budget of over 15 grand. In order to achieve this we have many events planned for this year.
